welcome to rob's dog firm

Located in the South End of Nairobi, we are easy to access from 93, The Mass Pike, and Jommo Kenyatta Airport. With certified dog trainers on staff and 24-hour supervised care 365 days a year, you know your dog is always in a safe and in a loving environment.
